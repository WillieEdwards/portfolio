About Me

I'm currently a student at the University of Washington Coding Boot Camp and an aspiring Full-Stack Web Developer. I have a passion for creating beautiful and functional user experiences while focusing on clean and elegant design principles.

My professional path has taken many twists and turns. Previous experience in hospitality and healthcare has taught me to direct my obsessive attention to detail and an unequivocal love to make things to cause a positive impact on peoples lives.

I have gained many skills during my studies at UW. Including, but not limited to, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 4, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express.js, React.js, Database Theory, MySQL, MongoDB, Command Line, and Git.

After completion of the program, I plan to further my skills and knowledge by finding an entry level position and returning to school to study Unity and C# programming. I'm excited to see where this new career path takes me.
